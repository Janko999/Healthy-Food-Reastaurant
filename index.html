Freshii's Peanut Butter

Indulge in the rich, wholesome goodness of our well-known homemade peanut butter, crafted with care and passion.

We start with premium, roasted peanuts, ensuring a deep, nutty flavor that sets our peanut butter apart. What makes it truly special is the simplicity of the ingredients - just peanuts and a touch of natural sweetness, without any additives or preservatives.

Freshii's Nutella Cream

Elevate your snacking experience with this heavenly spread – a delicious fusion of health and decadence, all in one jar.

We begin with premium, roasted hazelnuts, ensuring a robust and nutty foundation for our creamy creation. To enhance the nutritional profile, we incorporate natural sweeteners, such as honey or maple syrup, avoiding refined sugars and artificial additives. The addition of high-quality cocoa gives our Nutella cream its luscious chocolatey richness, without compromising on health-conscious choices.

Freshii's Chips

Experience the joy of guilt-free snacking with our homemade healthy chips, where flavor meets nutrition in every satisfying crunch.

Snack without the guilt and savor the satisfaction of a treat that aligns with your commitment to a healthy lifestyle. From movie nights to midday cravings, our homemade healthy chips are the ideal companion, offering a symphony of flavors that prove you can snack smart and deliciously. Elevate your snacking experience today!
